Privacy Policy

CADAC Corporation is committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal information while using our website. This policy applies to the information collected by us through our website.
I. Personal Information We do not collect personally identifiable information about you unless you voluntarily provide it on our website. You can browse our website without sharing any personal details. However, we collect information when you choose to contact us or use credentials from third parties like Facebook, Twitter, Google, or LinkedIn. This information may include your name, date of birth, email address, contact number, or address. The data collected is used to respond to inquiries, process orders, or develop services and solutions. Additionally, we gather non-personally identifiable information, such as device details, connection information, and web log data, to analyze trends and improve our services. We may share your information with other CADAC Corporation offices globally or with partners and third-party service providers acting on our behalf. These third parties are obligated to use your data solely for CADAC Corporation’s purposes. We may disclose your information if required by legal or regulatory authorities or as part of business reorganizations, ensuring appropriate protections are in place.
II. Use of Cookies Our website uses cookies, small text files that save and retrieve information about your visit. Cookies do not provide personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily supply it. We use cookies to understand site usage, enhance website content, and personalize your experience. You can configure your browser to deny or accept cookies. Note that disabling cookies may limit certain features on our website.
III. Security We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse. Information is stored on secure servers managed by CADAC Corporation or its authorized agents. However, the confidentiality of data transmitted online cannot be guaranteed, especially unencrypted data, which may be accessed by third parties. Please exercise caution when sharing sensitive information online, and acknowledge that website use is at your own risk.
IV. Links to Other Websites CADAC Corporation’s website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such external websites.
V. Access to Your Personal Information You may access, update, or correct the personal information we hold by contacting us at cadaccorp@gmail.com. You can also request the deletion of your data, which we will process within reasonable efforts. Note that complete removal of your data may not be guaranteed due to technical constraints.


VI. Authenticity CADAC Corporation is not responsible for verifying the authenticity of the information you provide. It is your responsibility to ensure that the information you share is accurate and reliable.
VII. Children Our website is intended for individuals in a professional capacity and is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or market data to children under this age.
VIII. How to Contact Us For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: CADAC Corporation Address: #77, JBR Tech Park, 6th Main Road, EPIP Zone, Whitefield, Bangalore Karnataka,INDIA Email: cadaccorp@gmail.com
IX. Grievance Redressal For grievances related to data processing, please contact cadaccorp@gmail.com. We will address complaints as quickly as possible.
X. Changes to This Policy CADAC Corporation reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected with a revised "last updated" date at the top of the page. Our commitment to protecting your privacy will remain unchanged.
